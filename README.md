# 🛒 Amazon Sales Analytics Project

## 📌 Project Overview

I worked with Amazon fashion sales data from 2022 to answer simple but important questions: where the revenue was coming from, why so many orders were canceled, and what growth opportunities were hiding in the data.

**Dataset:** 128,975 orders | ₹7.86 Crore revenue | 9 categories

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The seller had one year of sales history and needed a clear story from it.
They wanted to know:

- Which customer groups and products were driving revenue?
- What was causing so many order cancellations?
- Where should the business invest to grow sales?

---

## 🔍 What I Found

| Finding              | Why it matters                                                                  |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 🔴 High Cancellation | About 14% of orders were canceled, which is nearly double the normal rate       |
| 📅 Seasonal Risk     | Almost 70% of revenue was concentrated in Q2, leaving the rest of the year weak |
| 🗺️ Regional Gap      | North India sales lagged behind other regions                                   |
| 💰 B2B Opportunity   | B2B buyers were rare, but their orders were 66% larger than average             |

---

## 💡 What I Recommend

1. **Cut cancellations** by making size, returns, and product information clearer.
2. **Push more into North India** with targeted campaigns and offers.
3. **Build the B2B channel** with bulk pricing and tailored deals.
4. **Spread revenue across the year** with festival and off-season collections.
5. **Lift average order value** using bundles and complementary product suggestions.

**This could add ₹90 lakh or more to annual revenue.**
